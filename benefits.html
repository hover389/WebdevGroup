Hover News

Hoverclub members receive the official bi-monthly newsletter publication HOVERNEWS. It is sent free to each member 6 times per year and is made available on this web site for on-line viewing. HOVERNEWS' purpose is to advance the exchange of ideas. Club members may view an entire decade of historical archives of HOVERNEWS on this web site in PDF format.

Web site and Chat Forum

This web site is packed with information for those new to the sport of hovercraft, and looking to get started, as well as the veteran hovercrafters. Historic photos, videos, build diaries, event photos and summaries, upcoming events calendar, technical documents, links to valuable resources on the web and so much more is available to our members. Our chat forum is an invaluable resource for helping hovercrafters share ideas, and learn from others.

Technical Resources

Are you looking to build your first hovercraft? Club members benefit from the experience and expertise of our seasoned hovercrafters. Our members have built numerous hovercraft and can advice you on every aspect of a hovercraft build. Don't go it alone!

Organized Events and Racing

Do you enjoy sport and competition? Have you ever wondered how you could become a hovercraft racer? Hoverclub members enjoy sanctioned racing events held throughout the country. Hoverally, our largest event of the year usually combines racing, cruising, themed hover challenges, a banquet, camping, and all round fun over a 3-4 day weekend.

Casual outings and cruises

Think Hovercraft cruising is fun? It's twice as much fun cruising with a group of friends! Club members organize weekend cruise events and casual hover-ins throughout the year, from sunny river cruises in Florida, to lake cruises in Tennessee, to Winter ice hover-ins in Michigan and Wisconsin. Club members are sure to find a Hoverclub event within driving distance.

New and Used Hovercraft and parts

Hoverclub members have access to a complete listing of hovercraft manufacturers and providers of spare parts, as well as listings of used craft for sale.
